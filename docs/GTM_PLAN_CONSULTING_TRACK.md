- "We design and productionize typed memory systems for agentic applications."

Primary buyer:
- CTO / Head of AI / AI Platform Lead in mid-market and enterprise teams shipping copilots or internal agents.

Primary pains:
- Memory quality drift and hallucinated recalls.
- Lack of provenance/auditability.
